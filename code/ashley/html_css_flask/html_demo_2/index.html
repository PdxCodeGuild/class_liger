Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson was born on May 12, 1956. He works as a safety inspector/chemist at Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. He is married to Marge Bouvier and together they have 3 children: Bart, Lisa and Maggie. Homer and his family have lived in Springfield their entire lives. Homer was raised on a farm by his parents Abraham and Mona Simpson. Some of his favorite places include Kwik-E-Mart, Krusty Burger and Moe's. His favorite things to eat are hamburgers, hotdogs and doughnuts and he loves to top off his meal with a Duff Beer. His favorite TV character is Krusty the Clown and his best friend is Moe Sizlack. Homer is known for his emotional outbursts and is very envious of his neighbors, especially the Fladers family.
