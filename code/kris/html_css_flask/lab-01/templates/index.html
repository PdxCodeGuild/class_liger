Jules Winnfield

Jules Winnfield is a hired gun for Marsellus Walus. He is a religious man and frequently ends his monologues by saying "And I will strike down upon thee with great vengeance... And you will know I am the Lord when I lay my vengeance upon you." an adaptation of Ezekiel 25:17.
Current Residence:

Jules currently resides in Los Angeles, California
