The Life and Death of Barney

Everybody grows, and that's a *VERY* good thing we all do.
- Barney The Purple Dinosaur

Barney is an American Bred Purple Tyrannosaurus Rex. He was born in Dallas Texas to Sheryl Leach in 1988. However, he currently lives in all of our imaginations. Barney lived to convey educational messages through song and dance with a friendly and optimistic attitude. He fell ill on November 2nd, 2010. However, that didn't stop him from trying to make a comeback before his untimely passing in 2010. Rumours say that overwork played a major part in his short life. In his prime, Barney had a few Hit singles such as "Barney is a Dinosaur" and "I Love You". Despite being a carnivorous type dinosaur, Barney liked many foods like fruits and vegetables, but his Favorite is a peanut butter and jelly sandwich with a glass of milk.
